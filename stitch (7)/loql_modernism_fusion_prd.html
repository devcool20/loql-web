# Loql - Modernism Fusion ## Product Overview **The Pitch:** Loql bridges the digital and physical divide, turning anonymous streets into connected communities. It’s a hyper-local lending and sharing platform steeped in cultural warmth, designed to make borrowing a ladder as natural as asking for a cup of sugar. **For:** Urban dwellers feeling disconnected from their immediate surroundings who want to share resources, reduce waste, and build local trust. **Device:** desktop **Design Direction:** Indian Modernism Fusion. A marriage of warm handcrafted textures (Mitti noise, Paper Cream), structured utility (grid systems, Community Teal), and vibrant bazaar energy (stacked cards, Marigold Glow). **Inspired by:** Airbnb (trust mechanics), Paper Magazine (editorial layout), and traditional Indian block print aesthetics (patterns and warmth). --- ## Screens - **The 'Dwar' (Welcome):** Immersive entry point establishing warmth and local context. - **The 'Aas-Paas' (Discovery):** Dynamic, stacked-card marketplace of neighborhood items. - **The 'Katha' (Item Detail):** Story-driven product page with Polaroid visuals and owner notes. - **The 'Samvaad' (Checkout):** Conversational request flow ending in a virtual handshake. --- ## Key Flows **Borrowing an Item:** A neighbor finds and requests an item securely. 1. User is on **Aas-Paas** -> sees "The Loql Hero" featured items in a stacked grid. 2. User clicks an item card -> transitions to **Katha** screen. 3. User reads the handwritten note, clicks "Borrow with Love" -> opens **Samvaad** modal. 4. User selects dates, writes a greeting, confirms -> sees "Virtual Handshake" success state. ---
Design System ## Color Palette - **Primary:** `#E27D60` (Saffron Earth) - Main CTAs, warm brand moments - **Background:** `#FDFBF7` (Paper Cream) - Base page background - **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - Card backgrounds, modals - **Text:** `#2D3142` (Ink Dark) - Primary readability - **Muted:** `#8D99AE` (Stone Grey) - Secondary text, borders - **Accent 1:** `#41B3A3` (Community Teal) - Trust badges, verified ticks, secondary buttons - **Accent 2:** `#F64C72` (Marigold Glow) - Hover states, energetic highlights, notifications ## Typography - **Headings:** Lora, 700, 32-64px - Used for editorial headers and storytelling - **Body:** Cabinet Grotesk, 400, 16px - High legibility, slight geometric quirk - **Small text:** Cabinet Grotesk, 500, 14px - Eyebrows, tags, dates - **Buttons:** Cabinet Grotesk, 700, 16px - Uppercase, 1px letter spacing **Style notes:** Heavy use of 24px organic border radii. Backgrounds feature a subtle `url('noise.png')` overlay at 3% opacity for a tactile "Mitti" (clay) feel. Shadows are warm and diffused, not harsh black. ## Design Tokens ```css :root { --color-saffron: #E27D60; --color-teal: #41B3A3; --color-marigold: #F64C72; --color-paper: #FDFBF7; --color-ink: #2D3142; --font-serif: 'Lora', serif; --font-sans: 'Cabinet Grotesk', sans-serif; --radius-organic: 24px; --radius-pill: 999px; --shadow-warm: 0 12px 32px rgba(226, 125, 96, 0.08); --shadow-card: 0 4px 12px rgba(45, 49, 66, 0.04); --texture-mitti: url('/assets/noise-texture.png'); } ```

Screen Specifications ### The 'Dwar' (Welcome) **Purpose:** Warm, immersive onboarding that asks for location. **Layout:** Split screen. Left: 40% solid Paper Cream with text. Right: 60% dynamic Rangoli-inspired geometric artwork. **Key Elements:** - **Greeting Typography:** 64px Lora header "Discover your neighborhood's hidden treasures." - **Location Input:** 64px height, Pill shape (`--radius-pill`), 2px Saffron border, large map pin icon. - **CTA Button:** "Step Into Your Neighborhood", solid Saffron, Cabinet Grotesk 700, sits right-aligned inside the input. **States:** - **Loading:** Rangoli shapes gently pulse in Marigold Glow. ### The 'Aas-Paas' (Discovery Home) **Purpose:** Browsing available items and connecting with the community. **Layout:** 12-column grid. Top navigation. "The Loql Hero" spans top 8 columns. Waterfall stacked cards below. **Key Elements:** - **The Loql Hero:** Large feature banner, 24px radius, Community Teal background, 3D illustration of a neighbor. - **Stacked Item Cards:** White surface, warm shadow. Overlapping layout mimicking a physical bazaar. - **Category Pills:** Horizontal scrolling list. Marigold Glow active state. **Components:** - **Item Card:** 280x360px, White. Top 60% image (rounded 16px). Bottom: Item name (Lora 20px), distance (Teal tag). **Interactions:** - **Hover Card:** Rotates 2 degrees, slight Y-axis lift, `--shadow-warm` applies. ### The 'Katha' (Item Detail Page) **Purpose:** Tell the story of the item and its owner to build trust. **Layout:** 2-column layout (60/40 split). Left: Visuals. Right: Details & Action. **Key Elements:** - **Polaroid Frame:** Main image sits in a white frame, bottom heavy, tilted -1deg. - **Handwritten Note:** Pale yellow background (`#FFF9E6`), Lora italic font, mimicking a real note from the owner. - **Verified Neighbor Badge:** Community Teal, shield icon, absolute positioned overlapping the owner's avatar. - **Borrow CTA:** "Borrow with Love", solid Saffron Earth, spans 100% width of the right column. **States:** - **Unavailable:** CTA turns Stone Grey, text reads "Currently with a neighbor". **Interactions:** - **Click Image:** Expands to full-screen lightbox. ### The 'Samvaad' (Request/Checkout) **Purpose:** Finalize the request with empathy and scheduling. **Layout:** Centered modal over blurred 'Katha' background. 600px max-width, 24px radius. **Key Elements:** - **Date Picker:** Clean, minimalist grid. Teal accents for selected dates. - **Message Field:** Label reads "Say hi to your neighbor". 120px height textarea, Mitti texture background. - **Trust Banner:** Small footer in modal. "Your request is protected by Loql Promise." **States:** - **Success:** Modal collapses into a "Virtual Handshake" Lottie animation, dissolving into a Teal success checkmark. **Interactions:** - **Click Confirm:** Button shows spinning Marigold loader before success state.

Build Guide **Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3 **Build Order:** 1. **The 'Aas-Paas' (Discovery Home)** - Establishes the core grid, card components, and Mitti texture foundation. 2. **The 'Katha' (Item Detail Page)** - Defines the typographic hierarchy (Lora vs Cabinet Grotesk) and the Polaroid visual style. 3. **The 'Samvaad' (Checkout Modal)** - Implements form elements, states, and the crucial trust mechanics (Teal accents). 4. **The 'Dwar' (Welcome)** - Final visual polish with the bold split-screen layout and Rangoli artwork.
